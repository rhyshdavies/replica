Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Replica AI's virtual try-on technology, integration process, and how it can work for your brand.

How realistic are the try-on results?

Our AI produces studio-grade imagery that captures accurate lighting, shadows, and fabric texture. The results are virtually indistinguishable from a professional photoshoot.

What types of fashion does it work with?

Everything from streetwear to luxury. Our models handle a wide range of fabrics and styles, including casual wear, formalwear, activewear, denim, leather, silk, and more.

How easy is it to integrate?

Very. Replica AI is designed to work seamlessly with your existing e-commerce setup. Our team handles the onboarding so your developers can stay focused on what matters.

Do we need to provide 3D models of our garments?

No. Our AI works directly from your existing 2D product photography. Just upload your standard flat-lay or mannequin images and the system takes care of the rest.

Does the customer need to download anything?

Not at all. The entire experience runs in the browser, on any device. No app, no account, no friction.

Is customer data safe?

Absolutely. We are fully GDPR and CCPA compliant. Photos are processed in real time and are never stored. All connections are encrypted.

How long does it take to go live?

Most brands are up and running within days. The exact timeline depends on your catalog size and integration needs, but we move fast.

Can I track how try-on affects my sales?

Yes. We provide a dashboard with insights on try-on engagement, conversion impact, and return trends, so you can see the results for yourself.
