Frequently asked questions

Replica AI's VTON solution integrates with Shopify, Magento, BigCommerce, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud via a lightweight JavaScript SDK (<50kb) and REST API. Retailers see 22–30% fewer returns and 15% higher AOV. Setup takes under 48 hours for standard stores.

Integration & Technical

What platforms does your Virtual Try-On (VTON) integrate with?

Our VTON solution is platform-agnostic and integrates seamlessly with Shopify, Magento, BigCommerce, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud. We provide a lightweight JavaScript SDK and a robust REST API for custom-built headless commerce stacks.

How much latency does the virtual try-on add to the page load?

Our VTON widget is designed for performance. By using edge-computing and lazy-loading techniques, the core script is under 50kb. The 3D/AI garment rendering typically loads in under 1.5 seconds on a standard 4G/5G connection, ensuring zero impact on your Core Web Vitals.

Do we need to provide 3D models of our garments?

No. Our proprietary AI engine generates high-fidelity virtual drapes directly from your existing 2D product photography. You simply upload your high-resolution "flat-lay" or "ghost mannequin" images, and our system handles the 3D physics and mapping.

Business Value & ROI

How does VTON impact return rates and AOV?

Retailers using our VTON technology see an average reduction in returns of 22–30% by eliminating "size doubt." Furthermore, interactive engagement typically increases Average Order Value (AOV) by 15% as customers feel more confident in their purchasing decisions.

Can the VTON tool handle different lighting and skin tones?

Yes. Our neural rendering engine is trained on diverse datasets to ensure inclusive and accurate representations across all skin tones. The system also dynamically adjusts garment lighting to match the environment of the user's uploaded photo for a realistic "mirror" effect.

User Experience & Privacy

Does the user need to download an app or create an account?

No. Our VTON experience is entirely web-based and runs directly in the mobile or desktop browser. Users can see their results instantly without the friction of an app download or account registration, which maximizes conversion rates.

How is user data and privacy handled?

We are fully GDPR and CCPA compliant. User photos are processed in real-time for the session only and are not stored on our servers unless the user explicitly chooses to "save" their profile. All data transmission is encrypted via SSL/TLS.

Setup & Support

How long does the typical implementation take?

For standard Shopify or BigCommerce stores, setup takes less than 48 hours using our pre-built plugins. Custom enterprise integrations typically take 2–4 weeks depending on the complexity of the SKU library and custom UI requirements.

Does your AI support complex fabrics like lace, sequins, or leather?

Yes. Our engine simulates specific material physics, including the transparency of lace, the reflectivity of sequins, and the structural stiffness of leather, providing a high-fidelity visual experience that matches the physical garment.

What kind of analytics do you provide?

Our dashboard provides real-time data on "Try-On-to-Cart" conversion rates, most-tried-on items, and return rate correlations. We also offer direct integration with Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Adobe Analytics.
