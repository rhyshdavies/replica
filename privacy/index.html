Privacy Policy for Clueless

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Clueless ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Photos: To provide our core virtual try-on feature, the App requires you to upload a full-body photo (your "Digital Twin"). Garment photos you add to your closet are also collected. These photos are stored securely in Firebase Storage.
  • Authentication Data: You may sign in using Apple Sign In, which may share your name and email address with us (depending on your Apple ID settings). If you choose not to sign in, we use Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which does not collect personal identifiers.
  • Display Name: You may optionally provide your name during onboarding.
  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App (e.g., screen views, swipes, item interactions, try-on events) using Firebase Analytics. This data is used to improve our recommendation engine and user experience.
  • Pinterest Data: If you choose to connect your Pinterest account, we access your public boards and pins to import style inspiration. We do not store your Pinterest credentials; authentication is handled by Pinterest's OAuth flow.

2. Third-Party AI Data Sharing — Google Gemini

This section explains how your personal data is shared with a third-party AI service.

The App uses Google LLC's Gemini AI service (the "AI Service") to power its core features. Before any data is shared with Google, the App requests your explicit consent via a dedicated in-app disclosure screen. You must grant permission before any personal data is transmitted.

2.1 What Data Is Sent to Google

The following personal data may be sent to Google's Gemini AI servers when you use AI-powered features:

  • Your selfie / full-body photo (your "Digital Twin") — used for virtual try-on generation
  • Photos of clothing items you upload to your closet — used for garment digitization and analysis
  • Photos of clothing items used in virtual try-on — combined with your Digital Twin to generate try-on images

No other personal data (name, email, location, etc.) is sent to Google's AI service.

2.2 Who Receives Your Data

Your photos are sent to Google LLC (headquartered in Mountain View, California, USA), which processes them using its Gemini AI service on Google's servers. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf under its Gemini API Terms of Service.

2.3 Why This Data Is Sent

Your photos are processed by Google Gemini AI to:

  • Generate virtual try-on images — showing you wearing fashion items
  • Digitize garment photos — creating clean product images for your digital closet
  • Analyze clothing items — detecting category (top, bottom, shoes, etc.), color, and style metadata
  • Create outfit combinations — suggesting coordinated outfits from your wardrobe

2.4 How This Data Is Protected

  • All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS encryption
  • Google processes the images solely to generate and return results per their Gemini API Terms of Service
  • Your data is not used by Google to train their AI models when accessed via their paid API tier
  • We do not store your photos on any server other than your private Firebase account and Google's transient processing

2.5 Your Control Over AI Data Sharing

  • Consent required: You are asked for explicit consent before any data is sent to Google. The App will not send photos to Google without your permission.
  • Withdraw consent: You can disable AI data sharing at any time in the App's Settings. This will disable AI-powered features (try-on, digitization, outfit combos) but you can continue using the app.
  • Delete your data: You can delete all your data by deleting your account in the App's Settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Virtual Try-On: Your photo and garment images are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate virtual try-on images, as described in Section 2.
  • Garment Digitization: Clothing photos are processed by Google Gemini AI to create clean product images and extract metadata (category, color, style), as described in Section 2.
  • Image Preparation: Your photo may be processed by a server-side image segmentation service to crop and prepare it for virtual try-on. Only the photo is sent; no other personal data is transmitted.
  • Personalization: We use your style preferences and interaction data to recommend outfits and products tailored to your taste.
  • App Improvement: We use anonymous usage data to enhance our styling algorithms and user interface.

All third-party service providers listed above are contractually required, by their terms of service, to provide the same or equal protection of user data as described in this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data (photos, profiles, and closet items) is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud), which employs industry-standard encryption and security practices.
  • Photos and profile data are only accessible by you (the authenticated user).
  • AI-processed images are generated on-demand and stored in your private account.

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • When you delete your account, all associated data (photos, profile, closet items, saved outfits) is permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Anonymous analytics data is retained in aggregate form and cannot be linked back to you after account deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data (including photos, closet items, and saved outfits) at any time directly within the App's settings.
  • You can withdraw AI data sharing consent at any time in the App's Settings, which will disable AI-powered features.
  • You can disconnect your Pinterest account at any time.
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
